Research Highlights

Ionospheric Variability & Plasma Dynamics

Studies on ionospheric responses to geomagnetic storms, including plasma irregularities, Total Electron Content (TEC) variations, and radio wave propagation across different latitudes and longitudes.

Solar Flare & Space Weather Impacts

Analysis of solar flare events and solar transients on ionospheric structure, magnetic field perturbations, proton events, and their implications for communication, navigation, and space weather forecasting.

Advanced Solar Phenomena & Plasma Diagnostics

Spectroscopic and observational diagnostics of solar flares, surges, filaments, and cool loop footpoints, providing insights into plasma heating, self-absorption, and homologous surge behaviour.

Global Geomagnetic & Ionospheric Effects

Assessment of geomagnetic storms and solar wind influences on the ionosphere globally, including delayed responses of energetic particles and geomagnetic activity under solar cycle variations.
