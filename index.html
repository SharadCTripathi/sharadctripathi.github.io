About

Dr. Sharad Chandra Tripathi works in the area of ionospheric physics, space weather, and plasma dynamics with emphasis on computational modelling and radio wave propagation.

His research explores ionospheric irregularities, solar flare effects, radio wave propagation, and computational modelling of plasma processes.
