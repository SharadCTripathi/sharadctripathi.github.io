About

Dr. Sharad Chandra Tripathi is a physicist and researcher specializing in Solar Physics, Space Weather, Ionospheric Physics, and Space Instrumentation. He earned his Ph.D. from Barkatullah Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal, and was awarded the Erasmus Mundus INTACT Fellowship by the European Union. He completed post-doctoral research at Frederick University, Cyprus, and has extensive experience in developing scientific instruments, including contributions to India’s first Mars mission (MOM) and solar observation telescopes at PRL, Ahmedabad.

With a strong foundation in Advanced Plasma Physics and observational astronomy, Dr. Tripathi has designed and implemented systems to study galactic radio signals and ionospheric phenomena. He has authored multiple publications in international journals and presented his research at national and international conferences.

In addition to his research, Dr. Tripathi is an experienced educator and academic leader, mentoring undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral students. As the coordinator of Unnat Bharat Abhiyan at VIT Bhopal University, he facilitates the engagement of rural communities with the institution under this flagship programme of the Ministry of Education, Government of India, promoting sustainable development and rural empowerment.
